    About School

    • About
    • Mission
    • Vision
    A School Is A Temple For Learning. It Is The Crux Of An Individual's Life. A School Helps To Mould The Personality Of An Individual By Educating, Moralizing Coaching To Live Intellectually, Encouraging And Inspiring To Partake In Multifarious Activities. K.G.V.M Was First Established In 2006-07 And Was Renamed As R.N.V.K In 2017-18 Under "Yashaswini Education Society" With The Aspiration To Impact Quality Education To Budding Individuals. The School Was Started In A Campus Comprising Of Just Four Rooms And Hardly 35 Students. Today, The School Spans Across 50 Well Ventilated Spacious Classrooms Accomodating 1000 Students.
    To educate all students to the highest levels of academic achievement, to enable them to reach and expand their potential, and to prepare them to become productive, responsible, ethical, creative and compassionate members of society. Our vision is for each child to develop a curiosity of learning, discover their interests and grow in their love of learning. We also desire to have strong families through parent support/fellowship and skills training.
    RNVK believe that education should take place in a fully inclusive environment with equal opportunities for all and that all children should learn to value religious and cultural differences. Our school strives to be at the centre of the local community with positive and effective links to the wider and global communities. RNVK aims at providing exceptional care to children while fostering each child’s intellectual, social, physical and moral development in an academic-rich environment.

    Certified Teachers

    All the teaching faculty at RNVK are exceptional in their skills. Their guidance can lead your children on the right path to achieving their goals

    Special Education

    Every child is unique and we ensure that every student is given individual attention. The curriculum at RNVK is adapted to help every child realize his/her potential and emerge a winner.

    Book & Library

    A school library is a place where knowledge, inspiration, imagination, and creativity are nurtured. Academic support books, Non-Fiction and Fiction books are the hallmark of our library. We ensure that every child spends some time here every day.

    Sport Clubs

    Sports are as important for the all-round development of a child as academics. Sports inculcate a sense of responsibility, encourage team spirit, develop discipline and help the child learn to deal with success and failure equally.
